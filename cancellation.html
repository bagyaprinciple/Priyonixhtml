Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Thank you for choosing Priyonix for your training and career development. Please read our Cancellation and Refund Policy carefully before purchasing any course from our website.

1. Course Enrollment

When you enroll in a course through our website, you gain access to training materials, sessions, and related services as described on the course page.

Once payment is successfully completed, your seat in the course is reserved.

2. Cancellation Policy

Students may request cancellation of their course enrollment under the following conditions:

  • Cancellation requests must be submitted within 3 days of purchase.
  • The request must be sent through email or the contact form available on our website.
  • Cancellation requests made after the course has started or after accessing course materials may not be eligible for cancellation.

The company reserves the right to review and approve cancellation requests.

3. Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed under the following circumstances:

Eligible Refund Cases

Refunds may be issued if:

  • The cancellation request is made within the eligible cancellation period.
  • The student has not accessed course materials or attended training sessions.
  • Payment was made successfully but the course was not delivered due to technical or company-related issues.

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following situations:

  • After the course has started.
  • If the student has attended live sessions or accessed training content.
  • For change of mind after purchase.
  • Failure to attend scheduled sessions.
  • Any placement outcomes, as job placement depends on individual performance and market conditions.

4. Refund Processing

If your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days.
  • The amount will be credited back to the original payment method used during purchase.
  • Processing time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

5. Course Rescheduling

In some cases, instead of cancellation, students may request:

  • Transfer to another batch of the same course.
  • Rescheduling to a future training session.
  • Transfer to a different course of equal or higher value.

All rescheduling requests are subject to availability and approval by the management.

6. Company Cancellation

Priyonix reserves the right to cancel or reschedule any course due to:

  • Insufficient enrollment
  • Trainer unavailability
  • Technical or operational issues

In such cases, students may choose either:

  • A full refund of the course fee.
  • Transfer to the next available batch of the same course.
  • Transfer to a different course of equal or higher value.
